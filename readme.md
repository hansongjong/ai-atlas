0. Project Declaration (Absolute Context)

This project builds an AI knowledge and foresight website.

AI is treated not as a tool, but as a civilizational event.

The site analyzes AI from:

Civilizational perspective (AI as a historical event)

Scientific perspective (hardware & software evolution)

Industrial & economic perspective (governance, labor, production)

Builder perspective (founders, researchers, AI users)

The site is built from a Korean national context,
but must remain globally meaningful and applicable.

1. Core Hypothesis (Non-negotiable)

We hypothesize that within the next century,
a single corporation operating a single AI system
may become the dominant operator of global civilization infrastructure.

This may lead to:

A world without material scarcity

Universal access to production and creation

Full surveillance and control

Loss of traditional human autonomy

This site does NOT predict.
It documents irreversible choices and structural transitions.
